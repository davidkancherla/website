02

How it works

i.
It reads my world
Calendar, Gmail, Slack threads, my own notes. Claude / Codex have read access via tool use — pulls the live state of my day every time the dashboard refreshes.
ii.
It surfaces what matters
Aging customer replies. Meetings that need prep. Promises I made in last week's notes that haven't shipped. Decides priority by combining urgency, customer tier, and what I marked important.
iii.
It drafts the next move
One-click draft replies in my voice. Meeting prep notes pulled from related threads. A natural-language command bar so I can say "reschedule my 2pm" and it just happens.
